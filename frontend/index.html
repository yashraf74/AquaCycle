Our Purpose

Vision & Mission

Vision Statement

Vision Statement

Our vision is to become Egypt's leading digital platform for plastic and aluminum recycling, creating a seamless bridge between consumers and recycling factories. We aim to transform recycling into a simple, rewarding, and accessible daily habit for every household and business, contributing to a cleaner environment and supporting Egypt's Vision 2030 toward a circular and sustainable economy.

Mission Statement

Mission Statement

  • Our mission is to bridge the gap between consumers and recycling factories by creating a simple, accessible, and incentive-driven digital platform that enables the proper collection, sorting, and delivery of plastic and aluminum waste.
  • We aim to increase recycling rates, reduce environmental pollution, and support Egypt's transition toward a circular economy by supplying factories with clean, well-sorted materials while empowering individuals and communities to participate in sustainable waste management with ease and tangible rewards.

HOW IT WORKS

01

Collect Your Plastic

Gather your empty plastic bottles and aluminum cans. Every item you collect earns you cash rewards.

02

Choose Drop-off or Pickup

Either visit a TadweerHub collection point near you, or request a free home pickup directly from the platform.

03

Log Your Drop-off

Create your account and log how many items you've dropped off. Every bottle counts toward your balance.

04

Earn Cash & Track Impact

Earn points redeemable for cash payments. Track your environmental impact: CO₂ saved, water conserved, and more.

01

Register Your Factory

Create a factory account and specify what materials you need and your required quantities.

02

View Available Supply

See real-time inventory of collected, sorted plastic and cans available in your region.

03

Place a Supply Order

Request the quantity you need. TadweerHub handles collection, sorting, and delivery directly to your factory site.

04

Receive Sorted Materials

Get clean, quality-tested, well-sorted plastic and aluminum delivered on schedule at competitive prices.
